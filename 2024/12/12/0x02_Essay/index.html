0x02_Essay

发表于 2024-12-12 更新于 2024-12-13 分类于 英语 ， 英文练习

1，这种现象是多方面原因导致的。

This phenomenon is the result of many factors.

2，上述图表展示了 ….

The above chart shows that ….

3，他以专业第一而闻名。

He is renowned for being first in his major.

4，普天之下，莫非王土 ; 率土之滨，莫非王臣。

Under the heaven，all land belongs to the king ;

To the edges of the land，all are the king’s subjects.

  • submit to 臣服于

  • subject adj. 隶属的，臣服的

5，我们对现实的认识 : Our perception of the reality.

  • perception 认识，认知

6，人们纷纷推测她将辞职。

There was widespread speculation that she was going to resign。

7，文章结构 :

The reasons accounting for this phenomenon can be listed as follows.

For one thing , … （一方面）
For another , … （另一方面）
Besides, … (除此之外）

8，impetus

例句：The new government policy provided a strong impetus for economic growth.
翻译：新政府政策为经济增长提供了强大的推动力。

例句：Her speech gave the project an impetus to move forward with greater enthusiasm.
(en thu si asm)

翻译：她的演讲为项目注入了更大的热情和推动力，使其得以更好地推进。

例句：The discovery of renewable energy sources became the impetus for the development of green technologies.
翻译：可再生能源的发现成为绿色技术发展的动力。
