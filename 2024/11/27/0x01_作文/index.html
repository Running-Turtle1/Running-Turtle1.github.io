My View on Postgraduate Craze

Nowadays, pursuing postgraduate studies has become a trend. More and more college students are planning to continue their education at the postgraduate level.

But what has caused this phenomenon? In my opinion, the main reason is that there are so many undergraduates in China, making it difficult for them to find ideal jobs. As a result, many undergraduates choose to further their education at the postgraduate level in order to enhance their competitiveness in the job market.

What’s more, some students preparing for postgraduate exams are simply following others. They are unsure about their future plans, and seeing so many exam takers around them, they feel anxious and develop a herd mentality. As a result, they join the wave of postgraduate exam takers without clear goals.

In my opinion, we should not blindly follow others but instead pursue what truly interests us. If you are interested in scientific research and have the ability to devote yourself to it, you should go for it. However, in other situations, I think you should be cautious about your choice.

That’s all. I hope all college students can discover their true aspirations.

我对考研热的看法

如今，攻读研究生学位已成为一种趋势。越来越多的大学生计划在研究生阶段继续深造。

但是什么导致了这种现象呢？在我看来，主要原因是中国的本科毕业生数量太多，这使得他们很难找到理想的工作。因此，许多本科生选择继续在研究生阶段深造，以增强自己在就业市场中的竞争力。

此外，一些准备考研的学生只是盲目跟风。他们对未来的计划并不清楚，看到身边有许多考研者，他们感到焦虑并产生了从众心理。于是，他们没有明确的目标便加入了考研大军。

在我看来，我们不应盲目跟随他人，而是应该追求自己真正感兴趣的事情。如果你对科学研究感兴趣，并且有能力潜心研究，你应该去尝试。然而，在其他情况下，我认为你应该谨慎选择。

以上就是我的看法。我希望所有大学生都能找到自己真正的追求。
